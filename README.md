## 🛠️ Core Features

This toolkit includes specific tools for your academic workflow.

### Automated Literature Search
The tool connects to academic databases to find papers relevant to your topic. You enter your keywords, and the software builds a summary of top results. This saves you from browsing multiple sites to find specific files.

### Research Skill Integration
The software uses pre-built research skills to analyze text. It identifies gaps in your literature review and suggests areas for further investigation. It acts as an assistant for deep research tasks.

### Paper Writing Assistance
When drafting your paper, the toolkit provides structural templates. It suggests outline points based on your research notes. You retain full control over your writing, while the tool handles the formatting.

### Experiment Planning
Use this feature to organize your study design. The agent prompts you to list variables, methods, and expected outcomes. You can save these plans as structured notes for your lab records or grant proposals.

### Peer Review Support
The toolkit analyzes your draft text to find common errors or areas that need more detail. It matches your work against standard academic writing criteria.
